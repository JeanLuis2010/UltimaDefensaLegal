Call Center — How It Works

We connect paying members to local, independent attorneys. Calls are answered in English or Spanish, intake takes ~60 seconds, and you’re routed to a lawyer in your city.

  1. Call your city’s local number.
  2. Our system verifies membership by caller ID.
  3. Say your ZIP code and practice area (Immigration, Family, Employment, Criminal, Accidents).
  4. We ring local attorneys in your city (primary first, backups if needed). No busy signals.

Important

  • UDL is not a law firm; we connect you to independent licensed attorneys.
  • Membership covers intake/routing only — attorney fees are separate.
  • Calls may be recorded for quality/compliance. See Recording Notice.
