Attorney Participation Agreement

Effective Date:

This Attorney Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between Ultima Defensa Legal, Inc., a corporation incorporated under the laws of the State of Delaware ("UDL"), and you ("Attorney"). By agreeing to this document and submitting your initial payment, you confirm your intent to participate in the UDL platform and receive client referrals under the terms described herein.

1. Attorney Services

  • At least one (1) free or reduced-fee legal consultation per calendar month for each referred subscriber.
  • Document review for subscribers (up to 20 pages depending on membership tier).
  • Letters or phone calls on client’s behalf, as reasonably requested within subscription limits.
  • Response within 24–72 hours for urgent or time-sensitive legal issues.

2. Fees and Payments

  • Initial, non-refundable fee of $194.97 USD (equivalent to three months of service at $64.99/month).
  • After the initial term, Attorney may continue at $64.99/month or select a quarterly billing plan if offered.
  • Non-payment after five (5) business days of a renewal deadline may result in suspension or removal from the UDL directory.

3. Directory Listing Terms

  • Attorney will appear by ZIP code, language spoken, and area(s) of practice.
  • Listings are non-exclusive unless otherwise stated in a written agreement.

4. Professional Conduct

  • Attorney agrees to remain licensed and in good standing with the relevant state bar(s).
  • Attorney is solely responsible for legal services rendered to referred subscribers.
  • Attorney will comply with all applicable ethical, legal, and professional conduct standards.

5. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 15 days’ written notice. All prepaid fees are non-refundable unless termination is initiated by UDL without cause or due to platform discontinuation.

6. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and interpreted under the laws of the State of Delaware. Any disputes shall be brought before a court of competent jurisdiction located in Delaware County, Delaware.

7. Agreement Acceptance

By checking the box below and submitting your payment, you confirm that you have read and agree to the terms of this Agreement. You authorize Ultima Defensa Legal, Inc. to list your professional information and route referrals accordingly.
